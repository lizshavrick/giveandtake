What is give & take?

A physical space that houses the recycling of local artwork and exchange of creative waste, fostering the flow of ideas, inspiration, colors, words and collaboration.

&

A digital system that supplies you with local material guidance and a repository to help share give & take with your community.

Our project aims to help our community become more conscious creators.
