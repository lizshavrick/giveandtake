Material Guidance


give & take finds material guidance incredibly important in a maker's design practice. In consideration of our creative waste and use of potentially harmful products to the Earth and ourselves, give & take supplies creators with resources for alternative sustainable materials. If you are in NYC, you may email us if you would like to request a physical copy of our experimental zine, Recipes for Sustainability, otherwise it is available for download (but keep in mind, the file is 17.4 MB).
