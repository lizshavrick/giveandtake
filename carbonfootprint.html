Our Process



While sometimes it can feel like everything we make creates some sort of waste, it's important to remember that there is a balance to creating. We like to start our process in considering our balancing beliefs when it comes to making.

Our three pillars help guide us in creating consciously: accessibility, functionality and sustainability.

The following is how along our road of creating this proect, we've done best at keeping track of our process. We also provide you with a blank version of our Process Footprint Guide.
