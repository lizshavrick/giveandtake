What is give & take?

A physical space that houses the recycling of local artwork and exchange of creative waste, fostering the flow of ideas, inspiration, colors, words and collaboration of skills through exchange. A digital system that supplies you with local material guidance and the repository to create give and take in your community.

Why give & take?

To help our community become more conscious creators.

Hours & Location

The artwork recirculation closet is currently located at the City Reliquary Museum, located in Williamsburg at 370 Metropolitan Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11211 near the Bedford L metro station.

Open Saturday and Sunday 12 to 6 PM.
